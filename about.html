faqs

Frequently asked questions

What are the login hours?

Our operating hours and login hours vary depending on the day of the week. Please visit our website or contact us directly for our most up-to-date information on our hours of operation Thank you for choosing our bakery for all your baked goods needs!

What do i get my refund?

We take great pride in the quality of our products and the satisfaction of our customers. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your order, please contact us directly and we will work with you to address your concerns. If a refund is necessary, we will process it as quickly as possible using the original payment method. Thank you for choosing our bakery for all your baked goods needs!

How long it will take food to arrive?

The length of time it takes for your order to arrive may vary depending on a number of factors, including your location, the size of your order, and the delivery method you select. We offer a variety of delivery options to suit your needs, including standard shipping, expedited shipping, and local pickup.

Do you offer all types of bread at your bakery?

Our bakery offers a wide variety of breads to suit every taste and preference. We take pride in using only the highest quality ingredients and traditional baking techniques to create the most flavorful and authentic breads, from classic sourdough and whole grain to specialty loaves and artisanal breads. However, our selection may vary depending on the day and availability, so we encourage you to check our website or contact us directly to inquire about our current bread offerings. Thank you for choosing our bakery for all your baked goods needs!

What is cost of food delivery?

We offer a variety of delivery options to suit your needs, including standard shipping, expedited shipping, and local pickup. The exact cost of delivery will be calculated at checkout and added to your order total.

Are your products made fresh daily?

Yes, we take great pride in baking all of our products fresh daily to ensure the highest quality and freshest taste possible. Our team of bakers and pastry chefs use only the finest natural ingredients and traditional baking techniques to create our wide range of baked goods, from artisanal breads and pastries to cakes and pies. We also have strict quality control measures in place to ensure that every product that leaves our bakery is of the highest standard. Thank you for choosing our bakery for all your baked goods needs!
